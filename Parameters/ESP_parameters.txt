Parameters found at the top of the ESP code: 
- *ap_ssid : Name of ESP accesspoint 
- *ap_password : Password to ESP accesspoint
- colors[] : Array containing 5 colors used in the tests
- foodWords[] : Words used for first roud of reading test
- numbersWords[] : Words used for second roud of reading test
- complexWords[] : Words used for third roud of reading test
